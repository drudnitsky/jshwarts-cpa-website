About

I'm Jonathan Shwarts, a Certified Public Accountant specializing in strategic tax planning and compliance. For nearly two decades, I've been dedicated to helping my clients navigate the complexities of the tax code and build sound accounting foundations that support their long-term goals.

My journey through public accounting and a private family office taught me that effective tax strategy is not about a single transaction or filing. It is a continuous process—a year-round partnership designed to anticipate needs, adapt to change, and ensure your financial decisions are always working in your favor. It's about structuring your affairs to achieve maximum tax efficiency and avoid surprises.

This philosophy is the cornerstone of my practice. I serve as a long-term strategic advisor, moving beyond simple tax preparation to develop a deep understanding of your complete financial picture. This allows me to provide tailored strategies for a diverse clientele, including, but not limited to:

  • An investor seeking to optimize the tax implications of your real estate or equity portfolio.
  • A professional service firm owner requiring ongoing tax strategy and business structuring.
  • An individual planning for the future through the complexities of trust and estate tax.

My commitment is to provide consistent, forward-thinking advice that ensures you are not just compliant today, but strategically prepared for the years ahead. If you are seeking a dedicated tax partner invested in your long-term success, I invite you to connect.
